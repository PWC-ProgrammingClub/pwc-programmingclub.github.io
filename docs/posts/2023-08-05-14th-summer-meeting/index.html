We updated Professor Chen on the status of the club.

We told Professor Chen about the dire membership issues and about our plans for next semester’s content including

Professor Chen mentioned the PACISE competition. We will start talking about that this semester, though it looks like the competition takes place in the Spring. Professor Chen will send me some past competition stuff.
